Dear Love,

From the very moment I first saw you, my heart knew something extraordinary had happened. You were wearing that black top and those cute specs, and your smile—oh, that smile—melted my heart in an instant. I couldn’t look away. That single glance made me fall for you, and from that moment on, my eyes could only seek you. You were the only person in the room who mattered to me. Everything about you—your hair, your face, your smile—was simply perfect, like you were crafted just for me.

I remember wishing to God that day, with all the hope in my heart: “Please, let this girl be a part of my life. Let her be mine, forever.” And then something magical happened. We spoke. I was stunned, almost unable to believe that someone as incredible as you would talk to someone like me. From there, we became friends, and slowly, we opened our hearts to each other. Bit by bit, we fell in love. And let me tell you, loving you has been the most beautiful part of my life.

But love isn’t without its challenges, and we’ve had our share of ups and downs. We’ve faced difficult times, and we’ve fought. Yet, despite it all, one thing has always remained constant: my love for you. Through every fight, every tear, and every moment of doubt, my heart has held onto you, because you are the most important person in my life.

I appreciate everything you’ve done for me—the efforts you’ve made, the care you’ve shown. I see it all, and I cherish it more than words can express. I want you to know that I care for you just as deeply. I love you with all my heart, and I always will. I know I’ve made mistakes, and I’m sorry for them. Please forgive me, and love me more and more each day, as I do you. Because every single day, I fall in love with you all over again, and my love for you only grows stronger.

I dream of a future with you—a future where you’re my partner, my other half, my wife. I dream of building a life together, of sharing every joy and every challenge, of raising a family together and seeing you as the incredible mother of our children. I want to grow old with you, to create a lifetime of memories that we’ll cherish forever.

You are my everything, and I am yours. Always and forever.

With all my love,

Your Tanmay
